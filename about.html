Welcome to Blend!

The Blend smoothie bar is located in the Lilly Recreation Center. It provides a nutritious alternative to the other beverages served in the cafes around DePauw’s campus.

While the menu boasts several “Signature Blends,” the option to build your own is always enticing. Here are some of our favorite BYO Blends to make choosing your ingredients less daunting!

Pro Tip: Don’t be afraid to add chia seeds to your concoctions! The texture is about as perceptible as raspberry seeds – and one teaspoon of chia seeds will add two grams of fiber to your diet!
